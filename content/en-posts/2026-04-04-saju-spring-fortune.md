## Reading the Energy of Spring 2026

In the Five Elements (五行) system, **Wood (木)** energy reaches its zenith in April. Like seedlings pushing through earth, this season holds the energy of new beginnings and growth.

---

## Fortune by Element Type

### Wood (木) — The Leader's Energy
Those with strong Wood energy in their charts find April especially favorable. This is the ideal time to launch new projects or act on long-delayed plans.

**This month's focus:** Decision and action. Stop overthinking — do it.

### Fire (火) — Passionate Surge
Wood feeds Fire (木生火), amplifying your passion and emotions. Creative and artistic work shines now, but watch for impulsive decisions.

**This month's focus:** Channel passion with direction. Avoid emotional reactions.

### Earth (土) — Steady Recalibration
Wood suppresses Earth (木剋土), which may feel like friction. Use this as a signal to reorganize internally rather than pushing outward.

**This month's focus:** Defense is the best offense. Choose stability over risky ventures.

### Metal (金) — Patient Advancement
Metal waits its turn in Wood season. What looks like caution now will translate into major results by summer.

**This month's focus:** Prepare, then prepare more. Your opportunity is coming.

### Water (水) — Flowing Generosity
Water nourishes Wood (水生木). You'll find meaning in supporting others. Collaboration opens unexpected doors.

**This month's focus:** The more you give, the more returns to you.
